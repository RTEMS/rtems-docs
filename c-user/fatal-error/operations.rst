:ref:`InterfaceRtemsFatal`, :ref:`InterfaceRtemsShutdownExecutive`,
:ref:`InterfaceRtemsPanic`, :ref:`InterfaceRtemsFatalErrorOccurred`, or
:c:func:`exit()`.
